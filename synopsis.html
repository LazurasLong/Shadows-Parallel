I. Shadow wakes up late to receive his Pokémon from Professor Oak. As he arrives, he finds that all three of the starter Pokémon have already been taken by other Trainers. Professor Oak offers him a Riolu, along with the caveat that it has a problem. However, Shadow graciously accepts. The Riolu does not like Shadow at first, refusing to even enter its Pokéball, but the two of them begin their journey together.
